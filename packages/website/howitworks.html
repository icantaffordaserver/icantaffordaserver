How Pluto Works

Pluto combines the community aspects of Reddit with the face-to-face connection of Skype. Start video conversations with cool and interesting people like you.

Start by filling out your profile.

Tell us your interests and tell us what you want to talk about. Share your schedule and talk when it’s convenient for you. We want to know what it is that makes you unique. This is the first thing that other people on Pluto will see.

Receive a new introduction every day.

We'll introduce you to other members of the Pluto community based on your profile. Every morning, we will send you a new curated connection. When you see somebody you want to talk to, invite them to chat.

Turn a stranger into a new friend.

Engage in authentic face-to-face conversations online. Talk about life, talk about your favourite things, talk about what’s on your mind. Everybody on Pluto share one thing in common, a genuine desire to connect.
