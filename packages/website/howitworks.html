How Pluto Works

Step One

We want your Pluto to start with us getting to know you. Take Jane, for example. She likes crafting on rainy days. She’s interested in people’s stories, listening to old records, and travelling to foreign places. She joined Pluto because she wants to meet people from different places with similar mindsets. Start by telling us your passions and interests, the burning questions you want answered as well as when you’re free to chat.

Step Two

Now we that we know a little bit about who you are, we’ll take care of the heavy lifting. Based on your profile, we'll connect you with cool and interesting people you’ll want to have an amazing conversation with. You can check Pluto every day for curated introductions to other members of the community and pick a time that works for both of you.

Step Three

It's time to talk! Pluto is all about genuine human interaction - talk, laugh, cry and share over video chat with your conversation partner. Be open to where the conversation will take you. Share stories, exchange ideas, and see the world from a different point of view.
