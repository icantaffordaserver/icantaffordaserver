How Pluto Works

First, we at Pluto want to get to know you.

Tell us your interests, tell us what you want to talk about, and tell us when you want to talk. Meet Jane. She’s interested in people’s stories, listening to old records, and travelling to foreign places.

Next, we’ll introduce you to other people like you.

Jane wants to meet interesting people from across the globe. We’ll connect you to other members of Pluto based on your interests. On Pluto, you receive new, curated introductions, every day.

It’s time to connect and learn with someone new.

Pluto promotes face-to-face interaction and recreates real life. Jane and Pedro learn by sharing stories and exchanging ideas. On Pluto, you get to truly connect with people who want to hear what you have to say.
