Biography

Originally a student of clinical psychology, I graduated the University of Waterloo with a passion for coding. I took my very first computer science course halfway into my academic career, hoping to learn how to write some simple Python scripts to automate my data analysis workload. I soon realized that I enjoyed coding a lot more than I enjoyed my current field. From there, I used what I learned from Psychology and applied it to the field of visual and UX design. I was able to find a marketing job in tech, then a UX Designer position, eventually ending up as a Product Designer at a local startup.

In addition to my work interests, I game as much as I dance as much I love dogs. Although I've performed hip hop sets at a number of non-profit events, my gaming career is not as colourful. That being said, I'm still always down to meet new friends on Summoner's Rift!
